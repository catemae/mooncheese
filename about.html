about

hi, i'm cait. i love playing around with cheap film cameras and capturing moments in analog. all of my images are developed & processed by me in my attic darkroom. i enjoy the tactile experience of film and the little surprises that come with every roll.

this site, mooncheese, is my little corner to share my work, document my experiments, and connect with anyone who appreciates the imperfect charm of film photography.
